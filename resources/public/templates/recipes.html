Mom's World Famous Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 or 4 ripe bananas, smashed

Preparation

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Mix in the ingredients in a bowl. Add the flour last. Pour the mixture into a loaf pan and bake for one hour.
